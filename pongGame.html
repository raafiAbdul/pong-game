pong

This game has no bouncing physics, so bare with me here.

Rules

The goal for the "1 player" version is to hit the ball as many times as you can. Basically an endless and boring mode. If you miss the ball, your score gets reset to 0.


The "2 players" modes goal is kinda different. This gamemode has a 10 points limit, you have to block the ball from passing to your side. If you hit the ball and shoot it to the other side, then you get a point, same thing for the enemy. Lets be honest, if you dont know any of this, then your must be living under the biggest rock.

The controls for the one player is "w" and "s".
